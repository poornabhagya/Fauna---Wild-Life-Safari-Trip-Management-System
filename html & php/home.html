Welcome to FAUNA wildlife safari tours


For over 10 years, FAUNA Safari Tours has been serving clients from all over the world. We are dedicated to providing the highest level of comfort, enjoyment, and adventure as you explore Sri Lanka. Our staff includes driver-guides and tour designers with years of experience visiting Sri Lanka's wild habitats. Their expertise and intuition are vital in ensuring that each safari we provide is exceptional. There are numerous exciting wildlife tours to select from, and the most of them are suited for families. You'll see a lot of elephants, birds and leopards. You'll have plenty of opportunities to take lots of photos. You'll have a fantastic time here!
